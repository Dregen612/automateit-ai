### Recipe 1: Instant Lead Response
**What it does:** Drafts a personalized response to a new lead within 2 minutes
**Time saved:** 3 hrs/week
**Tools:** ChatGPT or Claude

**PROMPT:**
```
You are a sales assistant for [BUSINESS NAME]. A new lead just came in:
- Name: [LEAD NAME]
- Source: [WHERE THEY CAME FROM]
- Interest: [WHAT THEY'RE LOOKING FOR]
- Budget: [THEIR BUDGET IF KNOWN]

Write 3 personalized email responses in [YOUR VOICE/TONE - e.g. professional but friendly, casual, executive-level]. Each should:
1. Acknowledge them specifically
2. Address their stated interest
3. Include a clear next step (call/meeting/demo)
4. Be under 100 words each
5. Sound like a real person, not a template

Format as:
Option A: [email]
Option B: [email]
Option C: [email]
```

**SETUP STEPS:**
1. Copy the prompt above
2. Replace [BRACKETED TEXT] with your specifics
3. When a new lead arrives, paste into ChatGPT
4. Pick the response you like best (or combine)
5. Send within 2 minutes of lead arriving

### Recipe 2: Lead Qualification Score
**What it does:** Scores leads 1-10 so you focus on the hottest prospects
**Time saved:** 1 hr/week
**Tools:** ChatGPT or Claude

**PROMPT:**
```
Score this lead from 1-10 on their likelihood to buy. Be honest, not generous.

Lead info:
- [LEAD NAME], [TITLE] at [COMPANY]
- Source: [HOW THEY FOUND YOU]
- Their question/problem: [WHAT THEY SAID]
- Timeline mentioned: [WHEN THEY WANT TO BUY]
- Budget indicated: [IF STATED]
- Previous interactions: [PAST TOUCHES]

Consider:
- Are they the decision maker?
- Is their timeline immediate?
- Do they have budget?
- Did they come through a warm referral?

Respond with:
Score: X/10
Reasoning: [2-3 sentences on why]
Best next action: [call, email, nurture, etc.]
Priority: [High/Medium/Low]
```

**SETUP STEPS:**
1. Copy the prompt
2. Create a simple spreadsheet with columns: Name | Score | Priority | Next Action
3. For every new lead, paste into ChatGPT and record the score
4. Sort your leads by score — call High first
